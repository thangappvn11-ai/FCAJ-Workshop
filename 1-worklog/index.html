This worklog summarizes the weekly learning progress, hands-on AWS practice, and the VDCMS capstone project completed during the First Cloud AI Journey internship. The content below follows a concise weekly outline with links to each week’s detailed notes.

Info: Learning and hands-on journal across 12 internship weeks, from 17/04/2026 to 30/07/2026.

Over 12 internship weeks, I studied and practiced AWS services from foundational topics to advanced concepts, completed hands-on labs, and built the Voice-Driven Construction Management System (VDCMS) capstone project.

  1. Week 1 — Cloud Journey Kick-off — AWS Global Infrastructure, Amazon Q & foundational AWS services
  2. Week 2 — AWS Management Console, Gen AI on AWS, Cost Optimization, IAM & secure multi-layer VPC architecture
  3. Week 3 — Storage, networking essentials, CDN, security controls and account hardening
  4. Week 4 — Compute (EC2), storage (EBS/EFS/FSx), hybrid DNS, RDS and monitoring
  5. Week 5 — Organizations, Secrets Manager, Certificates, Terraform, Containers & CI/CD
  6. Week 6 — Developer tools, SAM, SSM, analytics, FinOps and interview prep
  7. Week 7 — Security posture, hybrid cloud, EFS, complete web architecture and AWS Meetup
  8. Week 8 — Streaming data, data warehouse, AI/ML and generative AI topics
  9. Week 9 — VDCMS requirements, database design, backend and frontend implementation
  10. Week 10 — VDCMS workflows: task assignment, voice reporting, chat and account management
  11. Week 11 — Deployment architecture, CloudFormation, speech pipeline, Cognito, SES and documentation
  12. Week 12 — CloudFormation validation, final deployment, end-to-end testing and cleanup
